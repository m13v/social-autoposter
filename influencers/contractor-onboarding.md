# Contractor Day-1 Onboarding

Send this once we hire someone on Upwork.

## Welcome message template

Hi [name], welcome aboard. Setup takes about 30 minutes per account. You'll be doing this from YOUR OWN phone, not a shared device. That's the whole point of the model.

**1. Confirm your kit**
- Smartphone you will use for posting (Android or iOS)
- Your home or mobile internet (no coworking VPN)
- Your personal email and personal Facebook (we will use these for the Business Manager invite later, NOT for the brand account)

**2. We send you the new-account creds**
- Email: `<account>@<our domain>` (we created this; you only need it for IG login the first time)
- Phone: `+1 555 ...` (we own this number; we forward the SMS code)
- Handle + brand brief: in shared Drive

**3. Create the IG account on YOUR phone**
- Install Instagram fresh (or use a clean profile)
- Sign up with the email we sent
- When IG asks for the phone, enter the number we provided
- Ping us in Slack / WhatsApp the moment you tap "send code"; we'll forward the SMS code within 60 seconds
- Finish profile setup using the brief

**4. Convert to Business right away**
- Settings, Account type and tools, Switch to professional account, Business
- Pick the category from the brief

**5. We add the account to our Meta Business portfolio**
- We do this on our side; takes about 5 minutes
- You will then get an email from "Meta for Business" with subject "[business name] invited you"
- Click the link, sign in with YOUR OWN personal Facebook
- Approve the invite

**6. From now on, post via Business Suite, NOT the IG app login**
- business.facebook.com or the Meta Business Suite app
- You are signed in as yourself; you pick the brand IG account from the dropdown; you publish
- You will NOT use the IG account's password day-to-day

**7. (If we also assigned TikTok)**
- Same shape: we send creds, you create the account on your device, we add it to TikTok Business Center, you accept the Member invite
- Day-to-day posting via TikTok Business Center

**8. First post test**
- I queue one easy test post in the Drive folder
- You publish at the scheduled time, paste the live link back to me
- This is paid. If it goes well, we continue.

## What I will give you
- Unique email + phone per account (and SMS forwarding when IG / TikTok ask)
- Slack / WhatsApp invite for daily handoffs and SMS relay
- Google Drive folder with the day's media, caption, and post time
- Posting SOP (1 page)
- Calendar invite for a 15 min sync if you want one

## What I will NOT give you
- The IG / FB / TikTok password day-to-day (you log in once at signup, then everything is via Business Suite as yourself)
- Access to anything outside the assets I have explicitly granted

## Red flags that end the contract same day
- Creating the account on a shared, cyber-cafe, or VPN device instead of your own
- Letting another person post from "your" account
- Logging into the IG account directly with the brand password instead of going through Business Suite after handover
- Missing the post window twice in 14 days without telling me first
- Editing captions without asking
