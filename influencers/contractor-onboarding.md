# Contractor Day-1 Onboarding

Send this once we hire someone on Upwork.

## Welcome message template

Hi [name], welcome aboard. Quick setup, takes about 10 minutes.

**1. Send me your IG and FB info**
- Your personal IG handle (so I confirm we are inviting the right person)
- The email tied to your personal Facebook (we will send the team-member invite there)

**2. Accept the Meta Business Suite invite**
- You will get an email from "Meta for Business" with subject "[business name] invited you"
- Click the link, sign in with YOUR OWN personal Facebook
- Approve the invite, you now appear in our People list
- You will see only the assets we explicitly grant you

**3. Accept the TikTok Business Center invite**
- Same shape, you get an email from TikTok
- Sign in with your personal TikTok or the email associated with it
- Approve

**4. Read the posting SOP**
- Linked in the shared Drive
- 1 page, 5 min read

**5. First post test**
- I will queue one easy test post in the Drive folder
- You publish it at the scheduled time, paste the live link back to me
- This is paid; if it goes well, we continue

## What I will give you
- Slack / WhatsApp invite for daily handoffs
- Google Drive folder with that day's media, caption, and post time
- Posting SOP (1 page)
- Calendar invite for a 15 min sync if you want one

## What I will NOT give you
- Our IG / FB / TikTok password (you do not need it; the team-member invite is how this works)
- Access to anything outside the assets I have granted in Meta Business Suite

## Red flags that get you ended same day
- Asking for our login credentials
- Posting from your own device but signed in as us instead of as a team member
- Missing the post window twice in 14 days without telling me first
- Editing captions without asking
