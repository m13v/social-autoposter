# Candidate Qualification Checklist

Quick-scan triage to apply to each Upwork applicant. Pass = shortlist. Fail any = decline.

## Hard gates (any miss = auto-decline)

- [ ] **Location.** Stated country in cover-letter Q1 is in NA (US, Canada) or Europe. Upwork-displayed country matches.
- [ ] **Sample task (Q7).** Walkthrough names actual menus / toggles ("Settings → Account type and tools → Switch to professional account → Business"). Generic phrases like "I would convert it to business" = AI cover letter, decline.
- [ ] **Team Member experience (Q3 or Q4).** "Yes" to having posted as a team Member (not as the brand owner) on Meta Business Suite OR TikTok Business Center. "I have my own IG with X followers" = wrong role, decline.
- [ ] **Own device.** Q2 confirms personal smartphone, not shared. No mention of "we have multiple devices in our agency" or similar.
- [ ] **No credential ask.** Did NOT ask us for the IG / TikTok password beyond initial signup. Asking = auto-reject (security violation).

## Soft signals (weight, don't auto-decline)

- [ ] **Time zone overlap with US Pacific 9am-9pm.** Europe-based: confirms OK with evening posting hours.
- [ ] **Pricing fit (Q6).** Accepts $500/mo per account at 130 posts. Counter > $700/mo or "I prefer per-post" = pass. Counter < $400/mo = caution flag (likely PH/LATAM lying about location).
- [ ] **Brand cadence handled (Q5).** Has done 2+ brands × ≥1 post/day for ≥3 months. New-to-this is OK if sample task is strong.
- [ ] **No scope creep.** Does NOT offer to "do strategy", "write captions", "create content", "manage DMs". This is execution only; offering more = misread the role.

## Red flags

- Generic agency intro with no tailoring to our JD
- "Upwork specialist" / "top rated" claims without specific Q7 answer
- Asks for off-platform contact (WhatsApp, email) before being hired
- Multiple typos in our key terms (calls Meta Business Suite "Facebook studio" etc.)

## Decision

- **All 5 hard gates passed + 2+ soft signals positive** → shortlist, send the day-1 onboarding doc
- **All hard gates passed + soft signals neutral** → reply asking for one clarifying detail (usually time zone or device confirmation)
- **Any hard gate failed** → decline with template "thanks, not a fit"
