Less than 30 hrs/week, very part time, expected ~1 to 2 hrs/day

## Experience level
Entry level

## Budget
Hourly: $5 to $10/hr, OR fixed at $3 to $5 per published post (your choice, mention which in your application)

---

## Job description

We are a US tech startup. We have an in-house content team that produces short videos and image posts for Instagram and TikTok. We need a reliable contractor to PUBLISH those posts on a daily schedule, NOT to create the content.

**What you will do**
- We give you a Google Drive folder with the day's media + caption (already written) + scheduled post time
- You log in to Meta Business Suite and TikTok Business Center using YOUR OWN personal account, after we add you as a team member (no password sharing, ever)
- You schedule or publish the post at the agreed time
- You confirm in our shared Slack / WhatsApp / Telegram that the post went out, with a link

**What you will NOT do**
- You will NOT write captions, design content, or shoot video
- You will NOT log in with our credentials, ever (we will not share them, and any contractor who asks for our password will be ended immediately)
- You will NOT respond to DMs or comments unless explicitly assigned

**Hard requirements**
- Reliable. Posts must go out within 30 minutes of the scheduled time. If you miss two scheduled times in a 14 day trial, we end the contract.
- Has used Meta Business Suite or TikTok Business Center before (mention which in your application)
- Available across at least one US time zone window (we post mostly between 9am and 9pm Pacific)
- English good enough to read and copy-paste captions correctly

**Trial**
- First 14 days is a paid trial at the rate above
- If we keep you, ongoing role at the same rate, with optional bumps for extra responsibilities (DM triage, reply ladders, scheduling tools)

**To apply**, answer in your cover letter:
1. Your time zone and typical working hours
2. Have you posted to a brand IG via Meta Business Suite as a team member before? (yes / no, brief detail)
3. Same question for TikTok Business Center
4. What posting cadence have you handled? (e.g., 3 brands x 2 posts/day for 6 months)
5. Hourly or per-post pricing? Which do you prefer for this role?

We will ignore generic AI-written cover letters. Mention the word "violet" in your application so we know you read this.
